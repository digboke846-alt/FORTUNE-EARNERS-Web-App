Frequently Asked Questions & Answers

1. What is Fortune Earners all about?

Fortune Earners is a digital web-earning platform that rewards users for completing simple online tasks, viewing sponsored advertisements, and referring new members.

2. How do I activate a plan?

Log in to your account, go to "Activate a Plan", choose your preferred plan and complete the payment instructions.

3. When can I withdraw my earnings?

Affiliate Wallet withdrawals are available daily from 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Task Wallet withdrawals are available on the 10th and 30th of every month from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM.

4. What is the minimum withdrawal?

The minimum withdrawal threshold for all Affiliate Wallet is ₦1,000, while the Task Minimum Withdrawal threshold is based on the the activated plan. All withdrawals are automatically processed.

5. Why is my withdrawal pending?

Task Wallet withdrawals require manual review before payment. Affiliate Wallet withdrawals are processed automatically once all requirements are met.

6. How do referrals work?

Share your referral link with others. When they register and activate a plan through your link, you earn a referral bonus based on their selected plan.

7. I forgot my password. What should I do?

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page to receive a password reset link.

8. How can I contact support?

You can contact us through our Telegram and WhatsApp support, by clicking Contact Support to message us. Our support team are always available.
